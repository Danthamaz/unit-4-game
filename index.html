Gold Rush!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four types of gold below. By clicking on a type of gold you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each image is hidden from you until you click on it.

All values will change for each new game.
